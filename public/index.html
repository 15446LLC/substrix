Reconciliation health

Every bank and credit card account with its last-reconciled date, color-coded — and transactions that were edited or un-reconciled after the fact, flagged with a drill-down to the exact entries.

Undeposited Funds hygiene

Payments sitting in Undeposited Funds that were never swept into a bank deposit, aged into buckets so you can tell processing lag from a months-old problem.

Unapplied payments & credits

Customer payments, deposits, and credit memos never applied to an invoice — and vendor credits and overpayments on the other side — so open AR and AP reflect reality.

Your data

  • Read-only. Substrix connects through Intuit's official QuickBooks Online API and never creates, edits, or deletes anything in your books.
  • Not stored. Your accounting data is read live each time you open the dashboard and discarded after the report is generated. We keep only your company's name and contact email so we can support you.
  • Visible only to you. Your financial data appears only in your own session, on your own screen. Because it's never stored on our servers, there is nothing for anyone else — including us — to view.
  • Not shared or sold. Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, used for advertising, or used to train AI models.
  • Revocable instantly. Disconnect from inside Substrix or from your QuickBooks connected-apps settings — either way, access ends immediately. Full details in our privacy policy.

How it works

  1. Click Connect to QuickBooks and authorize Substrix through Intuit's secure sign-in. No account creation, no setup.
  2. Substrix reads your accounts and transactions through the official QuickBooks Online API — it never creates, edits, or deletes anything.
  3. Your books-health dashboard loads in seconds. Reports are generated live on each visit; your accounting data isn't stored.
  4. Disconnect any time — from the dashboard or from QuickBooks — and Substrix's access is revoked immediately.
